🔒 Privacy Policy

PaisaTrack · Last updated: January 2025

📋 What We Collect

PaisaTrack stores only the data you enter — your name, salary, budget, and transactions. All data is saved locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. No data is sent to any external server.

🔒 How We Store It

All your financial information is stored exclusively on your device. We do not use any cloud database, analytics service, or third-party data collector. Your money data is 100% private.

🤖 Kiyara AI

When you chat with Kiyara, your messages are sent to OpenRouter's API to generate AI responses. We do not store these conversations permanently. Please do not share sensitive personal data (bank account numbers, passwords) with Kiyara.

🌐 Third-Party Services

  • OpenRouter API — for Kiyara AI responses
  • ElevenLabs TTS — for voice output (if enabled)
  • Google Fonts — for typography loading
  • Chart.js CDN — for charts rendering

🗑️ Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time using the "Clear All Data" option in Settings. This permanently removes everything from your device. You are always in full control of your data.

📧 Contact

For privacy concerns or questions, contact the developer: ashut1178@gmail.com
